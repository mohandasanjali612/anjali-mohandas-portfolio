Case Study

Strategic Patent Landscaping for
Graphene Enhanced Packaging

A deep tech startup needs to identify white spaces in the sustainable packaging market to ensure their graphene barrier technology doesn't infringe on existing patents and find areas for new filing.

The Problem

The graphene market is rapidly expanding, making it exceedingly difficult to track competitors. The client lacked visibility into where IP protection was most heavily concentrated and where opportunities for new, defensible innovation remained.

The Approach

Data Extraction: Systematically pulled all records related to graphene barrier coatings and polymers from global patent databases.

Categorization: Segmented patents specifically by technology application.

Trend Identification: Identified a distinct 5-year growth trajectory in bio-based graphene composites.
