Case Study

IP Legal Compliance & Strategy for
Quantum Technology

Managing complex intellectual property portfolios and legal compliance for the Wallenberg Centre for Quantum Technology (WACQT), bridging fundamental research with commercial patent landscapes.

The Challenge

Quantum technology represents a frontier market with highly complex, rapidly evolving IP landscapes. Navigating patent filings, structuring collaborative research agreements, and evaluating international legal compliance for emerging hardware and software protocols demanded a rigorous, proactive approach to avoid bottlenecks in technology transfer.

The Strategy

Portfolio Auditing: Conducted comprehensive reviews of existing quantum patent landscapes to identify defensible technological nodes.

Compliance Architecture: Established robust frameworks for university-industry collaborative research agreements, ensuring clear IP ownership and licensing provisions.

Strategic Navigation: Mitigated risks associated with overlapping IP claims from global quantum initiatives.
